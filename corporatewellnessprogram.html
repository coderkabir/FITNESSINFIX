FITNESS INFIXX’S GYM INTERNATIONAL

Fitness Infixx’s gym has been the authority in fitness since 1965 dating back to the original Fitness Infixx’s gym in Venice, California. It was the place for serious fitness. Opened long before the modern-day health club existed, the original Fitness Infixx’s gym featured homemade equipment and a dedication to getting results. It was an instant hit. Fitness Infixx’s gym quickly became known as “the mecca of bodybuilding.” In 1977, Fitness Infixx’s gym received international attention when it was featured in the movie pumping iron that starred Arnold Schwarzenegger and Lou Ferrigno. From that first gym in Venice, Fitness Infixx’s gym has become the largest co-ed gym chain in the world with over 701+ facilities in 30 countries. Today, Fitness Infixx’s gym has expanded its fitness profile to offer wellness programs which includes all of the latest equipment and services, including group exercise, personal training, cardiovascular equipment, spinning, and yoga, while maintaining its core weight lifting tradition. Fitness Infixx’s gym has become the preferred health and wellness gym of celebrities, athletes, bodybuilders, the military and fitness enthusiasts all over the world. Most importantly, Fitness Infixx’s gym continues to change lives by helping people achieve their individual potential.
